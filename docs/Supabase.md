# Getting Your Supabase Project URL and Service Role Key

Follow these steps to retrieve your Supabase project URL and service role key:

---

## 1. Get Your Supabase Project URL

1. Go to the [Supabase Dashboard](https://app.supabase.com/).
2. Select your project from the list.
3. On the project overview page, look at the top of the page for the **Project URL**. It will look like:

```
    https://your-project-ref.supabase.co
```

4. Copy this URL. You will use it as your Supabase Project URL in your application.

---

## 2. Get Your Supabase Service Role Key

1. In your Supabase project dashboard, click on **Project Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Go to the **API** section.
3. Scroll down to the **Service Role** section.
4. Click the **copy** button next to the Service Role key to copy it.

> **Warning:** The Service Role key has full access to your database. Keep it secret and never expose it in client-side code.

---

You now have your Supabase Project URL and Service Role Key ready to use for your backend or server-side configuration.
